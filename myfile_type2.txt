The trial was constituted with four test entries and two check varieties namely JRO 204 and JRO 524 and conducted over seven locations i.e., Barrackpore, Coochbehar, Kalyani, Katihar, Kendrapara, Naogaon and Rahuri. Location wise results are discussed below and fibre yield data are presented in table [Your Table]
Barrackpore: The trial was sown on 01.02.2011 at this centre and harvested on 01.08.2011 when the crop was 181 days old. Non significant difference was observed among entries.Check variety JRO 524 recorded high fibre yield (29.02 q/ha) followed by JRO 204 (28.82 q/ha), JROP-2 (28.03 q/ha), JROBA-3 (27.33 q/ha) and JROBA-4 (27.28 q/ha).
On the basis of pooled analysis over years, check variety JRO 524 recorded high fibre yield (32.82 q/ha) followed by NJ-7082 (32.37 q/ha), JRO 204 (31.53 q/ha), JROP-2 (31.29 q/ha) and JROBA-3 (30.87 q/ha).Entries were Non significant.
Coochbehar: Sowing of the trial was done on 01.02.2011 and the crop was harvested after 181 days on 01.08.2011. Highly significant difference was observed among the entries.Test entry JROBA-4 recorded highest fibre yield (29.21 q/ha) followed by JROBA-3 (28.81 q/ha) and NJ-7082 (28.1 q/ha) which is statistically on par with best check JRO 524 (27.98 q/ha) and JRO 204 (20.06 q/ha).
On the basis of pooled analysis over years, test entry JROBA-4 recorded highest fibre yield (33 q/ha) followed by NJ-7082 (30.98 q/ha) and JROBA-3 (30.97 q/ha) which is statistically on par with best check JRO 524 (27.74 q/ha) and JRO 204 (26 q/ha).Difference among entries was Highly significant.
Kalyani: Sowing of the trial was done on 01.02.2011 and the crop was harvested after 181 days on 01.08.2011. Highly significant difference was observed among the entries.Test entry JROP-2 recorded highest fibre yield (36.5 q/ha) followed by JROBA-3 (36.19 q/ha) which is statistically on par with best check JRO 204 (35.8 q/ha) and JRO 524 (27.07 q/ha).
Considering mean performance over years, test entry JROBA-3 recorded high fibre yield (35.38 q/ha) than the best check JRO 204 (35.24 q/ha). Followed by JROBA-4 (34.58 q/ha), JROP-2 (34.42 q/ha), NJ-7082 (31.32 q/ha) and JRO 524 (30.15 q/ha).Difference among entries was Highly significant.
Katihar: Sowing of the trial was done on 01.02.2011 and the crop was harvested after 181 days on 01.08.2011. Highly significant difference was observed among the entries.Check variety JRO 524 recorded high fibre yield (29.31 q/ha) followed by NJ-7082 (26.36 q/ha), JROP-2 (25.76 q/ha), JRO 204 (25.48 q/ha) and JROBA-4 (24.46 q/ha).
